Who we are

The Digital Interbank Network is the world's first blockchain-based, real-time B2B payments network operating entirely within the existing regulatory framework of the U.S banking system.

The Network is comprised only of FDC-insured banks
transaction real-time payment and performing other
banking services between their corporate clients-24
hours a day,7 days a week, 365 days a year, via
private permissioned blockchain.

The Network enables banks to offer a virtually
unlimited range of secure,real-time service to
their corporate client, including instantaneous
blockchain-based payment, Fedwire integration
and Smart Contacts.
